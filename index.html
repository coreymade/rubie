Meet Rubie

A fun addictive game of connections

Rack up those points

The object of the game is to collect as many Rubies as you can which turn to points. Connect 7 or more Rubies of the same color and clear the entire board of that color, and get the points too!

Worldwide Leaderboard

See who has the best Rubie chops. Your high scores are shared all over the world with other Rubie players. Compete to beat their scores and earn your Rubie bragging rights.
